Job Description

As a member of the Royal Mail Murder, you make sure that information, gifts, and important letters arrive on time, every time.

Take the leap and become the face and pride of the RMM! Watch this video for more information!

Responsibilities

  • Carry letters and small parcels during daylight hours
  • Collect outgoing mail to return to RMM sorting centers
  • Deliver mail within your assigned zone to the correct address

Application Steps

  1. Upload your resume
  2. Upload cover letter
  3. In person interview
  4. Practical exam (consists of the following)
    1. Sustained flight
    2. Mail sorting
    3. Aerial navigation
